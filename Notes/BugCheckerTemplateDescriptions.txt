MethodInvocationMatcher
======================
  + Matches based on a method called
  + you define a Matcher<ExpressionTree> for the type of method you're looking for that is being called
  + Example: AbstractReturnValueIgnored (is a template), ReturnValueIgnored


MethodTreeMatcher
=================
  + Matches based on the syntax of the method (method design) (not to be confused with the above...)
  + Examples include CheckReturnValue
